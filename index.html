Data Science + Visualization

I'm an astrophysicist turned data scientist and data visualization developer. After getting a Ph.D. in Astronomy & Astrophysics from Harvard, I decided to use my skills to help companies analyze and understand their data in order to create interactive tools that communicate, teach, and empower. My work is a mix of data science, analysis, and visualization and spans a wide range of topics such as sports, science, environment, education, government, and public policy.
