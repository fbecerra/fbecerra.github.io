Hi there!

My name is Fernando Becerra.

I tell stories with data.

I love analyzing datasets to discover what's hidden inside. Whenever I find one, I load it into Python and start exploring and digesting its information. Then I use d3.js to create interactive visualizations that engage and communicate those insights. My goal with this process is to empower people and motivate them to take action.

My interests span a wide range of subjects: science, politics, environment, education, and sports. My work is a mix of data science projects, visualizations, maps, photography, and writing. I also really enjoy taking pictures and being outdoors.
